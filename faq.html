Want to Ask Something from Us?

  • What's the ideal Solar System for my home?

    This depends on your energy needs, roof size, and other factors. Our team of solar experts can help you choose the right.

  • How long does solar installation take?

    For a 6.6kW system, it typically takes 2-3 days.

  • How to check solar system performance?

  • What's the warranty on the solar system?

    Generally, it comes with a 25-year performance warranty.

  • What's the total cost of the solar sytem?

    Our team of solar experts can provide a breakdown of the total cost.

  • How can Kangaroo help with going solar?

    With Kangaroo, you will get an experienced team and can be a part of 11,000 happy customers. You will get a customized solar system just for your home's needs. Our great warranty covers all the significant component repairs provided a regular maintenance (once in 2 years) of the solar system is done. We're with you for the long haul, not just the installation. Our installers are Clean Energy Council accredited (important for getting a rebate) and have installed over 1.2 million panels. We use top-tier, durable solar panels. You'll also have a personal solar advisor to guide you from the first research phase through installation and maintenance.

  • Do solar panel work during blackout?

    If your solar system is connected to the grid, it will shut off automatically during a blackout. You can get inverters with battery backups to provide power even when the grid is down.
