Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirt and grime build-up on photovoltaic panels can reduce energy production, so cleaning your solar panels is a great way to maintain maximum efficiency over the years.

Cleaning solar panels is fairly simple, however to ensure your safety and produce the best results for your system, you may want to get a professional solar panel clean. With all the right equipment and experience, we can provide you with a comprehensive solar panel cleaning service, along with expert advice to keep your solar system in the best condition in all weather conditions.

As a full-service solar provider, Kangaroo Solar offers a range of services that complement our solar panel systems for homes and businesses. While solar panel systems are typically low maintenance, it’s important to know that if something needs to be looked at, you can receive trusted advice on everything from installation to cleaning and maintenance.

What is a CEC accredited installer?

The Clean Energy Council is an independent body for the solar industry, providing training and accreditation in industry best practice standards to produce solar systems that are safe, reliable and meet customer expectations. Accreditation with the Clean Energy Council recognises individuals who have undertaken training to design and install solar, batteries and other renewable energy systems.

By purchasing a solar panel system for your home or business with Kangaroo Solar, you can access a highly qualified, professional team that can support you every step of the way.

  • Do I need a large roof for solar panel Cleaning?

    No, you don't need a very large roof. The number of panels you need depends on your energy consumption and available sunlight. Our team will conduct an assessment to determine the best fit for your space.

  • How much can I save with solar panels?

    Savings depend on your energy usage, the size of your solar system, and local electricity rates. On average, households can save 50–70% on their electricity bills after switching to solar.

  • Do solar panels work during a power outage?

    Traditional solar panel systems do not work during power outages unless they are paired with battery storage. With a battery, you can store excess energy to use during outages.

  • What is the lifespan of solar panels?

    Most solar panels come with a warranty of 25 years and can last even longer with proper maintenance. They typically lose a small percentage of efficiency over time.
